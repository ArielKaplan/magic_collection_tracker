## Long description ("About This Software")

[h2]Most collection trackers treat a Secret Lair as just another set code. This one doesn't.[/h2]

Mana Ledger models the [b]drop[/b] and the [b]deck[/b] as first-class products with a
purchase price and a knowable value — so it answers the questions collectors actually ask:

[list]
[*][b]Which drops made me money?[/b] Per-drop P&L: MSRP paid → current value → gain/loss, with real MSRPs synced from the community wiki.
[*][b]Should I crack this sealed drop or keep it?[/b] A singles-vs-sealed verdict on every drop you hold.
[*][b]Is the new drop worth buying?[/b] Upcoming drops with dates, prices, and the value math the moment they're announced.
[*][b]What am I missing?[/b] Completion bars on every drop and precon, one click to turn the gaps into a want list with price alerts.
[/list]

[h2]What's inside[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Secret Lair Explorer[/b] — every drop and superdrop ever released, finish-aware (non-foil, Foil, Rainbow Foil tracked as separate SKUs), with ownership, P&L, and the SL Index view of your holdings as an asset class.
[*][b]Precon Explorer[/b] — 1,500+ preconstructed decks from 1993 to today with full decklists, ownership, and "worth it?" pricing.
[*][b]Card Collection[/b] — table and gallery views, dual pricing (Scryfall low + TCGplayer market), price history sparklines, sold/realized-gains tracking, color and binder filters.
[*][b]Global search[/b] — type a card name and see everywhere it lives: your binders, your decks, the drops and precons that printed it, and the sealed products that contain it.
[*][b]Dashboard[/b] — drag-and-resize panels: collection value over time, realized gains, top movers, value by color/rarity/set, and more.
[*][b]Decks[/b] — track played lists with format legality and Moxfield / Archidekt / ManaBox / MTG Arena import & export.
[/list]

[h2]Your data stays yours[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Local-first.[/b] Everything lives in a single database file on your PC. No account, no cloud, no telemetry.
[*][b]Verified daily backups[/b] with one-click restore, built in.
[*][b]Free, forever.[/b] Mana Ledger is fan content — it can't be sold, and we wouldn't want to. If it earns a place in your toolbox, there's a Ko-fi link inside.
[/list]

[i]An internet connection is needed to refresh prices and card data (Scryfall, MTGJSON, TCGCSV); the app runs offline with its last-known data.[/i]
